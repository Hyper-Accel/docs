**LLM Processing Unit(LPU)** is designed for accelerating LLM inference. LPU architecture delivers high performance and energy efficiency compared to other accelerators such as NVIDIA GPU.

**HyperDex** is the E2E software stack dedicated for LPU. HyperDex empowers you to fine-tune your models and unlock the full potential of LPU technology. From enhanced power savings to robust processing capabilities, HyperDex brings flexibility and speed to your machine learning workflows.

**HyperDex API** compatible with HuggingFace framework, vLLM framework, and other custom frameworks. Whether you're working with models built in HuggingFace LLM models, HyperDex ensures a smooth transition to the LPU, allowing you to focus on innovation rather than infrastructure. 

**HyperDex SDK** offers everything you need in one stack—compiler, runtime, and drivers—providing a straightforward solution to integrate your pre-trained models into production environments. The unique efficiency of the LPU minimizes the need for extensive manual optimization, allowing you to accelerate deployment without compromising on performance.
